Dear Concerned Senior,

Welcome to our Brave New World.

Deep in the small print of Obamacare, the President and his advisors buried a piece of legislation that would help pay for his move to socialize medicine in our America.


 
He put a tax on medical devices. Essential LIFE-SAVING devices including defibrillators, insulin pumps and heart valves! Devices needed by the very sick. Appliances needed by the injured and maimed. Devices that directly affect older people. Like you and me!

He's crazy!

He claims that medical manufacturers are "rich enough to pay." Well, some may be. But every tax affects jobs, innovation and competitive advantage in the marketplace.

And the smaller companies are being clobbered. So they go out of business. And the bigger companies get bigger. And the choice of medical devices gets smaller as the prices increase. And you and I suffer.

Obama's Tax on Life-Saving Devices also punishes small businesses like doctors and dentists who have to pay it; as well as us - the end users.

So please help. Please complete the Senior American Association survey on Obama's Tax on Life-Saving Devices. We're going to lend out national support to Rep. Erik Paulsen, a Minnesota Republican, on his bi-partisan push to get this tax repealed.

We will send a message to Obama that the government must focus on jobs not taxes, and that he can no longer make the sick, the old and the disabled pay for his grandiose schemes. Robbing Peter to pay Paul will no longer cut it!

The U.S. is the only country that imposes an excise tax on medical-device companies. Let's make it stop!

Stand with Senior Americans Association as we take our fight to Congress, the President and the national media. Please use your voice to speak for those who can't.

Together we can defeat Obama's Tax on Life-Saving Devices! Let's defend our sick, our injured and our seniors - AND - let's help the U.S. economy! 

Sincerely,
 

 

Gilbert K. Davis, President
Senior Americans Association
