Dear Fellow American,

How can looting $1 TRILLION from Medicare possibly improve healthcare for America's senior citizens?

But that's exactly what Obamacare is going to do -- unless you and I stop it!

1. Please complete your STOP THE LOOTING OF MEDICARE EMERGENCY NATIONAL SURVEY. I need to know that you and I are fighting together to save this great nation -- and our senior citizens -- from the devastation of Obama's ill-conceived Medicare looting scheme.

Let me introduce myself. I'm Gil Davis and I'm a senior citizen. I'm also a former Assistant United States Attorney and I am still a practicing lawyer.

But today, I'm writing to you as an outraged American!

Obama and the far left in Washington have conspired to take over 1/6 of the U.S. economy -- and they've done it on the backs of some of our most vulnerable citizens -- America's seniors.

Now, you and I must fight back.

They think we are just going to roll over and take it.

That we'll let them get away with stealing a trillion dollars from Medicare. That we won't put up a stink. Or make them pay for throwing us under the bus.

But they are wrong -- so, so wrong!


You and I both know how hard it can be to find a doctor who will accept our Medicare or Medicare Advantage insurance.

The fees paid under Medicare are often too meager to make the added paperwork and bureaucracy worthwhile.

And this looting of the Medicare budget will only make it worse.

Already, thousands of doctors have stopped accepting Medicare patients. And that number is growing every day.

This is fast becoming a crisis -- a crisis that will impact every one of us in a terrible way.

To save our lives, you and I must stop them now!

Here at Senior Americans Association we have developed a plan to ensure I can deliver your views to Congress, the Obama Administration and the entire nation: 

1.  First, click here, I need your STOP THE LOOTING OF MEDICARE EMERGENCY NATIONAL SURVEY, today.

This is an ambitious plan -- even for Senior Americans Association. But I know that if we have your support, it can work. The stakes are high. 

We're going to need a lot of help from grassroots fighters like you. 

But I'm sure you understand that our future -- and our very health itself -- rests in the balance, and that DOING NOTHING IS NOT AN OPTION!

That's why I must ask you to immediately answer your STOP THE LOOTING OF MEDICARE EMERGENCY NATIONAL SURVEY. And once you have completed your SURVEY, please send your most generous contribution to Senior Americans Association.
